Best places to practice rafting in Brazil

Foz do Iguaçu (Paraná)

Did you know you can combine adrenaline and enjoy one of the most beautiful views in Brazil? This is possible in Foz do Iguaçu. The city is home to one of the most beautiful national parks in the country and is world famous for the Iguazu Falls. The current makes the region ideal for rafting.

The tour consists of a 4 km route — 2 km in calm waters and another 2 km in rapids that even form waves. Best of all, you can get very close to the waterfalls, which makes the adventure even more unforgettable.

Bonito (Mato Grosso do Sul)

Bonito is another famous destination both nationally and internationally. The city is formed by a rich ecosystem and there nature remains largely untouched. The rivers that bathe the region are suitable for rafting due to the currents, such as the Formoso River, which extends for up to 7 km where the traveler passes through 3 rapids and admires the beautiful landscape along the way.

Brotas (São Paulo)

Brotas, located about 220 km from the capital of São Paulo, is known as the adventure capital of São Paulo. Famous for being one of the ideal places to practice rafting, the region is home to the Jacaré Pepira river, considered one of the few that still remain clean and far from pollution.

Its fast rapids in the lower part form waterfalls of 3 and 4 meters. The adventure can also be done at night. There are several sports agencies that offer the tour.
