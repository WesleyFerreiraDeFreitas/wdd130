Types of Steel

Carbon Steel

Carbon steel is the most used for the production of knives and tools. This is due to its malleability after being heated and its hardness after being quenched.

Damascus Steel

Damascus steel is widely used because it is resistant and beautiful. It is forged using carbon steel parts and nickel steel parts. Then it is exposed to an acid that melts some of the steel and creates beautiful designs in the steel.

Inox Steel

Stainless steel is widely used because it is resistant to oxidation and always remains clean and shiny.
